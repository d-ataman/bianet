Bianet

A parallel news corpus in Turkish, Kurdish and English, collecting 3,214 Turkish articles with translations from the Bianet online newspaper.

If you use this corpus, please cite: Ataman, D. (2018) Bianet: A Parallel News Corpus in Turkish, Kurdish and English. In Proceedings of The Second Workshop on Multi-Language Processing in a Globalising World.
